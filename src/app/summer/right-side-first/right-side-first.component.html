We are all created special with different talents

God has a plan for everyone and we are all an individual member of the body of Christ. You are a masterpiece as you grow more and more as a Christian you will truly see how special and unique God created you.Jeremiah 29:11:"For I know the plans I have for you,"declares the LORD, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future."
